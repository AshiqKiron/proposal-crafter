= Can I use this plugin with my current theme?= 
Yes, Proposal Crafter will work with any kind of theme.

= Can I customize the appearance of my proposals (e.g., add logos, change colors)? =  
Yes! You can modify templates using the built-in Gutenberg editor. Changing color or logos or even adding other elements is possible.

= Does this plugin support e-signatures? =  
Yes, clients can digitally sign proposals (upload signed Image or directly Type), making approval seamless.   

= Are notifications sent when a client views or accepts a proposal? = 
Yes, notifications are sent to you whenever a client opens/views/accepts/declines a proposal. It's a pro feature of this plugin.

= Are proposals stored securely on my server or third-party services used for storage? =
All your proposal data are stored in your WordPress. You own every piece of information on your proposals/clients. No need to pay to keep/access your data.
